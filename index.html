Hello, My name is Jaesun Lee and I teach art lessons. My primary language I teach is in Korean and I have experience teaching visual arts in Korea. When I arrived in Canada I decided to continue but target younger children. It can be a difficult process to find joy in drawing at a young age, especially without someone to direct you. Art lessons were suprisingly uncommon in Canada compared to Korea I decided to start my own.
